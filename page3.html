Important dates through our time at Cushman (hover over)

April 2003 - Michael meets my parents for the first time. Mike came up to the cabin for the first time, and my parents were in the screened in porch waiting for him with there poker faces on.

April 2005 - Wendy and Michael are married. This was the start of our married with children. Mike and I had been coming to the cabin for years before we were married.

August 2005 - Eva Rose is born. Eva Rose was our first born. The cabin is the first spot that she slept through the night.

May 2009 - Hana Rae is born. Hana Rae rounded out our family. She also said the cabin was one of her favorite spots.

April 2010 - Purchase of our boat. We purchased a boat so we could enjoy time on the lake. Lots of memories of fishing and cruising.
